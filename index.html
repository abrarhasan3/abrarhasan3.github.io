01 / About

Three lives,
one mission —
make ideas land.

I have spent the past decade in three distinct rooms: the lecture hall, the research lab, and the debate stage. Each one has taught me something the others could not.

From Notre Dame College to KUET, and now an MSc at BUET, my path through computer science has been guided by one question: how do we make complex things — cryptographic protocols, intrusion detection, the mathematics underneath them — feel learnable to the next person who needs them?

That question shapes everything. It is why I run an educational YouTube channel, why I led the KUET Debating Society as president, and why my classroom at Green University looks the way it does. Teaching is not a fallback from research — for me, it is research's most honest test.
